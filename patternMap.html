Welcome to the pattern tracker for the custom joystick-controlled keyboard


Please use your joystick to attempt to replicate the shape you see in the screen. You will attempt each shape 10 times.
We are trying to understand how would you make this shape in your day-to-day. The more relaxed you are while drawing, the better!

Questionnaire


1) It was easy to replicate the shape with the given setup (joystick and computer).

2) I think I could perform this movement with my day-to-day setup as well as I did with the setup used during the study.

3) Replicating the shape required me to perform an uncomfortable movement, or a movement I could not do consistently.
